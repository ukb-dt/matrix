Σ as Matrix (cooperation within, transaction between)

Variance–covariance matrix, 5×5. Diagonal (variance) encodes within-agent cooperation; off-diagonals (covariance) encode between-agent transactions.
A B C D E
A σAA σAB σAC σAD σAE
B σBA σBB σBC σBD σBE
C σCA σCB σCC σCD σCE
D σDA σDB σDC σDD σDE
E σEA σEB σEC σED σEE

Canon: Diagonal (Var) marks cooperation within an agent—ordered signal-transduction that improbably maintains order against physics (Schrödinger’s “negentropy”). Off-diagonals (Cov) mark transactions between agents—synchronies that arise at interfaces. Collisions at h(t) stress Σ; the observed ΔS is what gets written into θ′.

Tip: rename A–E to concrete agents (e.g., Donor, Nondonor, Provider, Payer, Regulator) or subsystems within one organism if you’re modeling intra-agent structure.
